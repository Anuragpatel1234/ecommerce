# Comprehensive Client Requirement List: Rangaara Website Finalization

Please provide the following information and assets to complete the development of the Rangaara Fashion E-Commerce platform.

---

## 1. Branding & Business Information
- **Official Brand Name:** Full legal name for invoices and footers.
- **Brand Logo:** High-resolution logo files (SVG or Transparent PNG). Need both Dark and Light versions.
- **Brand Story:** Final text for the "About Us" and "Our Story" pages.
- **Social Media Links:** Full URLs for Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and Twitter/X.

## 2. Contact & Support Details
- **Customer Support Phone:** International format (e.g., +91 XXXX XXX XXX).
- **Official Email Address:** For customer queries (e.g., support@rangaara.com).
- **Physical Address:** For the footer and "Contact Us" page.
- **Business Hours:** When is customer support available?

## 3. Product & Media Assets
- **Product Images:** High-resolution images for each product (Minimum 1200x1600px recommended).
- **Category Banners:** High-quality landscape images for category headers (Shop, New Arrivals, Collections).
- **Hero Banners:** Stunning images for the homepage slider (minimum 3-4 images).
- **Lifestyle/Lookbook Images:** Any professional photoshoot images for the "Collections" sections.

## 4. Currency & International Settings
- **Conversion Service:** Choice of API (e.g., Open Exchange Rates) or specific fixed rates for USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, SGD.
- **Rounding Rules:** Do you want prices like $49.99 or rounded to $50?
- **Primary Currency:** Confirming INR is the base currency for all backend calculations.

## 5. Shipping & Delivery Features
- **Domestic (India):**
    - Confirm the "Free Shipping" threshold (Currently ₹2,000).
    - Standard delivery timeline (e.g., 3-5 business days).
- **International Shipping:**
    - Detailed shipping rates for Zone A (USA/Canada), Zone B (Europe), and Zone C (Other).
    - Delivery timelines for international shipping (e.g., 7-14 days).
- **Order Tracking:** Which shipping partners are you using? (Bluedart, DHL, FedEx, etc.) We need their API keys if real-time tracking is required.

## 6. Payment Methods & Gateways
- **Payment Gateway Accounts:** We need access/API keys for the following:
    - **PayPal:** Client ID and Secret for international payments.
    - **Domestic Gateway:** Razorpay, PayU, or CCAvenue keys.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD) Policy:**
    - **Domestic:** Maximum order limit for COD (e.g., ₹10,000).
    - **International:** Specific list of countries where COD is allowed.
    - **Verification:** Do we need to add an automated "OTP verification" for COD orders to prevent fake orders?

## 7. Tax & Legal Compliance
- **Tax (GST):** 
    - Confirm 18% GST for all apparel.
    - Should taxes be **Inclusive** (included in product price) or **Exclusive** (added at checkout)?
- **International Tax:** Confirm that international orders are Zero-Rated (No GST), but subject to local Customs Duty paid by the customer.
- **Legal Pages:** Final text for:
    - Return & Refund Policy
    - Shipping & Delivery Policy
    - Terms & Conditions
    - Privacy Policy

## 8. Marketing & CRM
- **Newsletter Service:** API keys for Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or similar if you want automated emails.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Do you want a "Chat on WhatsApp" button on the site? If yes, provide the number.
